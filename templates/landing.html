About DB-Genie

DB-Genie is a revolutionary AI-powered tool designed to streamline database management. By leveraging the power of large language models like Gemini, it allows you to interact with complex databases using simple, everyday language. Forget memorizing SQL syntax; just ask, and DB-Genie delivers.
